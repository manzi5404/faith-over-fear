Frequently Asked Questions

How does the sizing work?

We aim for a modern, sized streetwear fit. If you like it baggy or medium we got you. If you want a more standard fit, size down one. Check our Size Guide on each product page for measurements.

When will I get my order?

Can I return items?

Yes! We accept returns on unworn items within the same day of delivery. If it doesn't fit right, we'll swap it out. (Sale items are final sale).

How do I wash my F>F gear?

To keep the puff print popping and the fabric fresh: Wash cold, inside out. Hang dry is best, or tumble dry low. Do not iron directly on the graphics!

Do you restock sold-out items?

Some core items restock, but most Drops are limited edition. Once they sell out, they typically stay sold out. Sign up to get early access on our limited and other drops.
